AIR JORDAN - I

"Released in 1985, the Air Jordan 1 marked the beginning of a sneaker phenomenon, designed by Peter Moore for Michael Jordan's rookie season. Known for its striking silhouette and innovative features, the Air Jordan 1 combines premium leather with a high-top design for exceptional support. Its initial release was met with controversy due to its defiance of NBA uniform codes, leading to the 'Banned' moniker and boosting its legendary status. Available in various classic and modern colorways, the Air Jordan 1 remains a symbol of both athletic excellence and streetwear culture."
